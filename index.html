ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The Conference primarily focuses on providing international consortium to the Scientists, Researchers, and Practicing Engineers from both Academia and Industry to share their creative ideas on cutting-edge technologies. This global forum disseminates the technical innovations in theoretical concepts, methodologies, and applications in the fields of Electrical, Electronics and Communication, Computing and Information technologies, Mechanical, Materials and Manufacturing, Automation, Industry 4.0, Instrumentation, Control Systems and Biomedical Engineering. The conference will incorporate , Keynote sessions, invited Tech-talks, and Oral presentations in various fields of Engineering and Technology for the exchange of research findings.
